🚀Electric Vehicle Market Analysis in India | Strategic Insights for AtliQ Motors

This project focuses on analyzing the rapidly growing Electric Vehicle (EV) market in India to support AtliQ Motors’ expansion strategy. As a data analyst, the objective is to uncover key market trends, identify high-performing regions, and provide actionable insights to improve market penetration.

🔍 Problem Statement:
 AtliQ Motors, a leading EV manufacturer from the USA, holds a strong position in North America but has less than 2% market share in India. The company aims to expand its presence by understanding customer demand, regional performance, and competitive landscape.

📊 Key Objectives:
Analyze total EV sales across states and years
Identify top-performing states and manufacturers
Compare category-wise performance (2-wheelers vs 4-wheelers)
Track quarterly sales trends and growth patterns
Provide strategic recommendations for market expansion

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used:
Excel (Data Cleaning & Preprocessing)
SQL (Data Extraction & Transformation)
Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

📈 Key Insights:
Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu lead EV adoption
2-wheelers dominate the Indian EV market significantly
Certain manufacturers (like Ola Electric & TVS) hold major market share
Consistent growth observed across quarters, indicating rising demand

💡 Business Recommendations:
Focus expansion on high-growth states
Invest in 2-wheeler segment for faster market capture
Strengthen dealership & charging infrastructure in Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities
Build competitive pricing strategies based on regional demand

🎯 Outcome:
 This project demonstrates end-to-end data analytics skills — from data cleaning to dashboard creation — and delivers actionable insights to support real-world business decisions.
